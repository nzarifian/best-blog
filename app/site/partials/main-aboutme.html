Hello, World!


My name is Naz, and I love to travel. All my life I was determined to climb the corporate ladder. Curious and searching for something else, I took a break not knowing what lay ahead. I traveled solo, began surfing and sank deeper into my yoga practice.


Through the years, I developed a meaningful personal practice that would change my whole direction in life. I embraced a new happier, healthier lifestyle. I trained as a yoga teacher so my skills allow me to work or volunteer anywhere around the world. The travelers I meet on the road inspire me to live life to the fullest everyday.


Recently, I lived in an little surf island in Australia. In between yoga sessions, I played tour guide and took travelers to secret spots around the south coast and just chilled with the locals and expats.


I am currently traveling Europe, mostly volunteering as a yoga teacher for one year.


Hope you enjoy the journey ... Naz xx
